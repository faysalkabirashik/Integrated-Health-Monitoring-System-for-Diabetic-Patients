Your Diet Recommendations

Based on your input, we recommend the following diet plan:

  • Carbohydrate intake: Aim for 45-60 grams of carbohydrates per meal, and 15-30 grams per snack. Choose complex carbohydrates such as whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Protein intake: Aim for 0.8-1 gram of protein per kilogram of body weight per day. Good sources of protein include lean meats, fish, eggs, dairy products, and plant-based options like beans and lentils.
  • Fat intake: Aim for 20-35% of daily calories from fat. Choose healthy fats like avocado, nuts, and olive oil.
  • Hydration: Drink at least 8-10 glasses of water per day to help regulate blood sugar levels.
  • Fiber intake: Aim for 25-30 grams of fiber per day from sources like fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes.
  • Sugar intake: Limit added sugars to less than 10% of daily calories. Choose natural sources of sugar like fruits and vegetables instead of sugary drinks and desserts.
  • Meal frequency: Eat 3 main meals and 2-3 snacks per day to help regulate blood sugar levels.
